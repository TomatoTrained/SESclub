About Us

The Student Economics Society (SES) at Midlands State University is committed to fostering a deeper understanding of economic principles among students. Through diverse programs and activities, SES bridges the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world applications, creating a vibrant and inclusive community of aspiring economists.

Vision

To inspire and empower future economists to shape a sustainable and inclusive global economy.

Mission

To provide a platform for students to deepen their understanding of economics through experiential learning, collaboration, and mentorship, preparing them for real-world challenges and opportunities.

Core Values

At SES, we are guided by the following principles:

Academic Excellence:

Striving for the highest standards in education and research.

Integrity:

Ensuring honesty and transparency in all our activities.

Inclusivity:

Welcoming all economics students, regardless of academic background.

Collaboration:

Promoting teamwork and partnerships within the society.

What We Do

SES offers a variety of activities designed to enhance learning and professional development:

Guest Lectures & Seminars:

Gain insights from industry leaders and experienced academics.

Research Training & Projects:

Participate in hands-on research to apply economic theories.

Economic Debates & Case Study Competitions:

Develop critical thinking and analytical skills.

Workshops:

Learn economic modeling, data analysis, and personal finance strategies.

Networking Opportunities:

Build connections with peers, faculty, and industry professionals.

Economic Blog:

Contribute to our blog, exploring and discussing contemporary economic issues
